Who we are

We back the best talent
at the AI × Climate frontier

CLAI is a venture capital firm that invests in seed-stage startups building AI technologies to address climate mitigation and adaptation challenges, as well as startups improving AI energy efficiency.


We believe the best talent of our time is converging on AI × Climate, driven by breakthroughs in AI and the urgency of the climate challenge. Our founding team came together at Stanford's Distinguished Careers Institute, with deep ties to Stanford, MIT, ARPA-E, and Google.
