The team

Built at Stanford.
Invested in the planet.

Our founding team came together at Stanford's Distinguished Careers Institute, united by a shared commitment to applying AI for a more sustainable future.

Ajay Gupta
Ajay has been a venture investor in deep tech and climate tech startups in the valley for the last 10 years. He co-leads the Climate and Sustainability SIG at Stanford's Distinguished Careers Institute. Ajay had a career in equity trading for over two decades and ran the Equity Trading business of Bank of America Merrill Lynch in Asia Pacific. He co-founded NZVC, an early-stage VC fund in New Zealand.
LinkedIn
Jon Carter
Jon has been a senior partner at Egon Zehnder International for over 30 years. He founded their Technology Practice in the US and established the Silicon Valley office. Jon has recruited board members, CEOs and senior executives for companies including Alphabet, Intel, and Microsoft. He is now working with the Doerr School at Stanford promoting sustainability in the boardroom.
Stanford DCI
Radhika Malpani
Radhika was a Senior Engineering Director at Google from its early days for over 20 years, starting with leading Google Ads in 2000. She founded Google Images, growing it to over 1 billion queries per day, and co-led Google Travel Search. A prolific climate investor, Radhika co-teaches entrepreneurship at Stanford including Hacking for Climate and Lean Launchpad.
LinkedIn
Kirk Liu
Kirk served as a Technology to Market Advisor at the Advanced Research Projects Agency-Energy (ARPA-E), where he helped prepare innovative energy technologies for transition from agency-funded lab to commercial market. Kirk's career has focused on commercialization and growth of early-stage startups.

Cassandra Xia
Cassandra was a software engineer at Google Research and Google AI for 5 years before moving to the climate sector. She co-founded workonclimate.org, a 20,000+ member climate work movement. She now works on grid interconnection at Pearl Street Technologies.

Ty Jagerson
Ty has decades of experience as a sustainable energy executive. He started the Google X Tapestry project, a grid energy DERS technology, led V2X at GM Energy, and led business development at Xerox PARC. Board member at Phoenix Energy and UC Merced.
