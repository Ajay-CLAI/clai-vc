Meet The Team

Ajay Gupta
Ajay has been a venture investor in deep tech and climate tech startups in the valley for last 10 years. He co-leads the Climate and Sustainability SIG at Stanford's Distinguished Careers Institute. Ajay had a career in equity trading for over two decades and ran the Equity Trading business of Bank of America Merrill Lynch in Asia Pacific. He co-founded NZVC, an early stage VC fund in New Zealand.

Jon Carter
Jon has been a senior partner at Egon Zehnder International over the last 30 years. He founded their Technology Practice in the US and established the Silicon Valley office. Jon has recruited Board members, CEOs and senior executives for companies such as, Alphabet, Intel, Microsoft and a host of earlier stage growth companies. He is now working with the Doerr School at Stanford promoting sustainability in the boardroom.

Radhika Malpani
Radhika was a Senior Engineering Director at Google from its early days for over 20 years, starting with leading Google Ads in 2000. She founded Google Images, growing it to over 1B queries/day. She also started Google Local Search, and co-led Google Travel Search. A prolific climate investor, Radhika co-teaches entrepreneurship classes at Stanford including Hacking for Climate and Sustainability, and Lean Launchpad.
